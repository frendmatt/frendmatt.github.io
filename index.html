My Hobbies

Freerunning

Freerunning is the use of movements taken from gymnastics, capoeira, martial arts tricking, and parkour and using them to creatively navigate an environment. I have been training freerunning for the past eight years, and it is one of my hobbies I am most passionate about. The friends I have made from this sport are easily some of the closest friends I still have today.

Bouldering

Bouldering is a type of rock climbing in which you climb shorter, yet more challenging, routes without using any equipment. I like to boulder in order to stay healthy and strong.

Cubing

I fell in love with solving three dimensional puzzles back in high school. Since then my collection of puzzles has grown quite large. I've managed to solve the original rubiks cube in 11 seconds, as well as in a handstand.

Longboarding

I love to longboard around bike trails. I find it enjoyable to take in nature while feeling a breeze in my face as I ride around either a path or my college campus.

Photography

Although, I don't have a professional camera, I love to take photos using my phone cameras.
